Portfolio

EXAECHO

EXAECHO is an innovative platform designed to help students efficiently prepare for exams. Users can upload and share past exam papers, and the system generates similar questions to enhance their study experience. The platform promotes collaboration by enabling students to access a wide variety of resources and practice materials. EXAECHO provides a seamless, user-friendly experience, helping students improve their exam readiness through shared knowledge and intelligent question generation.

Edyfra

Edyfra is a platform that connects students based on their academic strengths and weaknesses. By analyzing students' results, Edyfra pairs individuals who can benefit from each other’s knowledge. The platform fosters collaborative learning, helping students to improve in areas where they may struggle. With an emphasis on personalized learning and peer support, Edyfra aims to enhance academic performance through meaningful partnerships.

Weather App

The Weather App provides accurate, real-time weather forecasts for locations worldwide. With an intuitive interface, users can easily check current conditions, view forecasts, and explore detailed weather data. Built with JavaScript and OpenWeather API, the app offers a seamless, user-friendly experience to help users stay informed and prepared for the day ahead.
